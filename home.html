Oceania Hostels

"The ocean is a mirror reflecting the world." Here at the oceania hotels, we make sure students reflect upon the best version of themselves , thriving to further improve with our help to lead a better and healthy lifestyle....

Hostel lifestyle,

Students will lead a healthy lifestyle with all the essentials taken care of so they can focus solely on their studies. Staff is made sure the environment is highly friendly and non-biased. The Vibes around the campus help nourish creative minds and nourish future geniuses...

Rooms and Living,

The rooms are specially designed so that even if the students are sharing rooms there will still be a sense of individuality. Students have the option of rooms shared between two, three, or four students, with everyone getting individual furniture for storage and sleeping...

Sports

Sports are a necessary part of a student's life where one has a chance to chase one's dreams physically. Sports are also good for releasing stress as well as a great way to be healthy while having fun. For students, the hostel provides sports grounds for Football, Cricket, Basketball, volleyball, etc. as well as indoor sports like chess, table tennis, etc. to nourish both body and mind...

Gym

With the new generation on the rise, the gym has become an essential part of living and is one of the necessities for healthy living. The gyms provided are high-end with the latest facilities and new machines for more convenient exercise. The gym size is big enough for students to share and still work out together without any issues...
